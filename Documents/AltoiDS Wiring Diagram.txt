   ====================== Power System =========================

   LiPo Battery (3.7V 2000mAh)
        |
        ↓
   [Toggle Power Switch] ← Physical on/off
        |
        ↓
   +-----------+
   |  TP4056   | ← USB Micro (for charging)
   | Charger   |
   +-----------+
        |
        ↓ (3.0-4.2V)
   +-------------+
   |  MT3608     |
   | Boost Conv. | → Adjustable to 5V output
   +-------------+
        |
        ↓ (5V regulated)
   +-------------------+
   | Radxa Zero 3W     |
   | ILI9341 Display   |
   +-------------------+

   ====================== Audio System =========================
   
   Radxa Audio Out (GPIO Header or 3.5mm if present)
        |
        ↓
   3.5mm Panel-Mount Jack → Headphones

   ======================= Notes ===============================
   - All 10kΩ resistors are pull-ups (GPIO reads HIGH when 
     button not pressed, LOW when pressed)
   - TP4056 charges LiPo when USB is connected
   - MT3608 must be adjusted to output exactly 5V before 
     connecting to Radxa
   - Toggle switch provides hard power cutoff
   - All grounds MUST be tied together (common ground rail)
   - GPIO 18 (PWM) allows brightness control for display 
     backlight
   - SPI must be enabled in Radxa config before display works
